Size & Fit Tips

Get the Perfect Fit Every Time

Follow these simple tips to ensure maximum comfort and choose the right shoe size with confidence.

Measure Both Feet

One foot may be slightly larger than the other. So use the larger measurement when selecting your shoe size.

Wear Your Regular Socks

Measure your feet while wearing the socks you usually wear with your shoes for the clear best fit.

Measure in the Evening

Feet naturally expand throughout the day, so evening measurements provide the most accurate results.

Leave Toe Space

Leave about a thumb's width of space between your longest toe and the front of the shoe for more comfort.
